Frequently Asked Questions

What does the commercial version offer?

Commercial versions include: commercial license (avoiding AGPLv3 compliance requirements for infra/minio modules), warranty and bug fix guarantee, custom offline packages, exclusive support from industry-leading experts for complex issues, technical support with response time SLA, and broader Linux/PostgreSQL version support.

How are nodes counted?

Node count refers to the number of distinct IP addresses managed by Pigsty.

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely! The open-source version is fully functional and identical to commercial versions. Evaluate thoroughly before deciding. We also offer a 30-day enterprise trial.

How do I get invoices and contracts?

We provide standard invoices and business contracts. Contact rh@vonng.com for commercial details.

What payment methods are supported?

We accept wire transfer, PayPal, and major credit cards.

What if I need more nodes?

Additional nodes beyond your plan can be purchased separately. Enterprise plans also support custom configurations based on your actual needs.
